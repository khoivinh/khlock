### Mobile Drag-and-Drop Refinements
- Replaced `PointerSensor` with `MouseSensor` — PointerSensor handles mouse+touch+pen via Pointer Events API, conflicting with our custom TouchSensors. This was the likely source of unpredictable drag behavior on mobile.
- Lowered tile body long-press delay from 600ms to 500ms to match iOS/Android OS-level long-press convention
- Added `touch-action: manipulation` on draggable tiles to reduce browser interference during long-press
- Added pre-activation visual feedback (subtle background highlight) on touch devices during the long-press wait
